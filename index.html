WELCOME TO PRAIRIE FIELDS!



This is the homepage of the Prairie Fields Home Owners Association (PFHOA), an organization consisting of and dedicated to helping owners of property in the Prairie Fields subdivision located in Savoy, IL. Here you will find material about the PFHOA as well as the neighborhood itself, such as information on the board and its members, announcements, frequently asked questions, as well as the various phases, committees and covenants that make up Prairie Fields. Choose a link from the menu above to get started!
